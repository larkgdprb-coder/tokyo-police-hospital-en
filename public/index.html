Important Notices

Apr 1, 2025 Important

As of April 2025, an initial consultation surcharge of ¥7,700 (tax included) applies to patients without a referral letter from another medical institution. Please bring a referral letter when visiting for the first time.

Apr 1, 2025 Notice

Afternoon outpatient clinics (except Pediatrics) are by appointment only. Please contact the Repeat Appointment Center at 03-5343-5620/5621.

Jan 1, 2025 Notice

MyNumber card (Individual Number Card) can now be used as proof of health insurance at reception. We encourage all patients to bring their MyNumber card.

Oct 1, 2024 Notice

Tokyo Police Hospital has been designated as a "Referral-Focused Medical Institution." We kindly ask patients who regularly visit for stable conditions to consider consulting their local clinic as their primary care physician.
