Thank you very much for using our hospital. We will resume our aroma outpatient clinic for pregnant women, which has been suspended, from April 2026.

During pregnancy, your body and emotions undergo major changes, and it is also a time when you can easily feel tired and anxious. At our outpatient clinic, we use aromatherapy (essential oils) that are safe for pregnant women and provide a relaxing time.

Please feel free to use it as a support for refreshing and relaxing your mind and body.

We look forward to your visit.
