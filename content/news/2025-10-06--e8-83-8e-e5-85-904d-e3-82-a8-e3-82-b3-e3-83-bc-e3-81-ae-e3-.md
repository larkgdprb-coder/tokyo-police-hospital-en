At our hospital's obstetrics and gynecology department, we have a time on Monday afternoons where you can watch a 3D video of your unborn baby. Although we are not an outpatient clinic for medical diagnosis, our clinic's clinical laboratory technicians who are proficient in ultrasound examinations will be in charge of your visit, and you and your family will be able to watch your cute baby move, and you will also be able to take home a USB with a 10-minute video recording of the day. Both those who are undergoing prenatal check-ups at our hospital and those who are visiting other hospitals can use our services, so please check the [Obstetric Information] → [Various Classes/Breastfeeding Outpatient] page for more details, and then consider using our service.
