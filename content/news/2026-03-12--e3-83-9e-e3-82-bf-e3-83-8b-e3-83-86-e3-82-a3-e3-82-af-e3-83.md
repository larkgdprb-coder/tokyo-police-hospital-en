Thank you very much for using our hospital.

From June 2026, couples can now participate in maternity classes. Until now, this was only for pregnant women, but now husbands (partners) can also participate so that couples can learn together about childbirth and childcare and have peace of mind during childbirth. Please join us as an opportunity to deepen our understanding of childbirth and childcare together.

[Reservations] Reservations are required. For more information or to apply, please contact reception or call us.

We look forward to your participation.
