## Overview

The Gastroenterology Department provides comprehensive care for diseases of the esophagus, stomach, intestines, liver, gallbladder, and pancreas. We perform a wide range of endoscopic diagnostic and therapeutic procedures.

## Key Services

- Upper GI endoscopy (gastroscopy) and colonoscopy
- Diagnosis and management of colorectal cancer and GI tumors
- Liver disease management (hepatitis B & C, fatty liver, cirrhosis)
- IBD (Crohn's disease, ulcerative colitis) management

## Clinical Focus

- Gastric and esophageal cancer screening and early detection
- Colorectal cancer screening and polypectomy
- Chronic liver disease and hepatocellular carcinoma surveillance
- Inflammatory bowel disease
- Peptic ulcer disease and H. pylori eradication
- Pancreatitis and pancreatic disease
- Gastrointestinal bleeding evaluation and treatment

## Procedures

- Upper endoscopy, colonoscopy, capsule endoscopy
- ESD (endoscopic submucosal dissection)
- ERCP (endoscopic retrograde cholangiopancreatography)
- Liver biopsy and ascites drainage
