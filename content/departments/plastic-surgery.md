## Overview

The Plastic & Aesthetic Surgery Department provides both reconstructive and aesthetic surgical procedures. Our reconstructive services focus on restoring form and function following trauma, cancer surgery, or congenital conditions.

## Key Services

- Skin cancer excision and reconstruction
- Post-mastectomy breast reconstruction
- Wound care and scar revision
- Aesthetic procedures (consultations available)

## Clinical Focus

**Reconstructive:**
- Skin cancer (basal cell carcinoma, squamous cell carcinoma, melanoma) removal and reconstruction
- Breast reconstruction after mastectomy
- Burn care and scar management
- Wound closure and skin grafting
- Congenital abnormalities (cleft lip/palate, vascular malformations)
- Post-trauma facial reconstruction

**Aesthetic:**
- Consultations for cosmetic procedures
- Eyelid surgery (blepharoplasty)
- Lipoma and benign skin lesion removal
