Our **10 specialized centers** bring together multidisciplinary teams to provide integrated care for specific conditions:

| Center | Description |
|---|---|
| **Stroke Center** | Rapid diagnosis and treatment of cerebrovascular events |
| **Blood Purification Center** | Hemodialysis and related therapies |
| **Cancer Center** | Comprehensive cancer diagnosis and treatment coordination |
| **Cardiac Center** | Advanced cardiovascular diagnosis and intervention |
| **Robotic Surgery Center** | da Vinci Xi surgical system for minimally invasive procedures |
| **Diabetes Center** | Multidisciplinary diabetes management and education |
| **Breast Center** | Dedicated team for breast cancer screening and treatment |
| **Perinatal Care Center** | Obstetric and neonatal care |
| **Palliative Care Center** | Pain management and comfort care |
| **Radiation Therapy Center** | Linear accelerator and brachytherapy |

## About Our Departments

Each department is staffed by board-certified specialists with expertise in their respective fields. We actively pursue accreditation from major medical societies and maintain continuous education programs for our medical staff.
