## Overview

The Cardiology Department provides advanced diagnosis and treatment of all forms of heart disease. As part of the Cardiac Center, we offer catheterization, electrophysiology, and interventional procedures, as well as comprehensive outpatient cardiac care.

## Key Services

- Coronary artery disease diagnosis and percutaneous coronary intervention (PCI)
- Heart failure management and cardiac rehabilitation
- Arrhythmia evaluation and catheter ablation
- Valvular heart disease assessment

## Clinical Focus

- Acute coronary syndrome (heart attack, unstable angina)
- Heart failure (systolic and diastolic)
- Arrhythmias including atrial fibrillation
- Valvular heart disease
- Hypertensive heart disease
- Cardiomyopathy
- Pericardial disease

## Diagnostic & Interventional Capabilities

- Echocardiography (transthoracic and transesophageal)
- Cardiac catheterization and coronary angiography
- Percutaneous coronary intervention (PCI/stenting)
- Cardiac electrophysiology and ablation
- Pacemaker implantation
- Holter monitoring and treadmill stress testing
