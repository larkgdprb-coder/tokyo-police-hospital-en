## Overview

The Psychiatry Department provides diagnosis, treatment, and ongoing care for a wide range of mental health conditions. We offer both outpatient and inpatient psychiatric care with a multidisciplinary team approach.

## Key Services

- Outpatient and inpatient psychiatric care
- Pharmacological and psychotherapeutic treatment
- Crisis assessment and management
- Liaison psychiatry for medically ill patients

## Clinical Focus

- Depression and mood disorders
- Anxiety disorders and panic disorder
- Schizophrenia and psychotic disorders
- Bipolar disorder
- Obsessive-compulsive disorder (OCD)
- Post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD)
- Adjustment disorders and stress-related conditions
- Sleep disorders

## Important Note

Outpatient psychiatric consultations require a **prior appointment**. Please call the main reception to schedule.

New patients are generally accepted by **referral** from another physician or healthcare provider.
