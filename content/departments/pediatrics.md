## Overview

The Pediatrics Department provides comprehensive medical care for children from newborns through adolescence. We offer both general pediatric care and specialist consultations, as well as neonatal support as part of our Perinatal Care Center.

## Key Services

- General pediatric outpatient consultations
- Neonatal care and support (in collaboration with Perinatal Care Center)
- Pediatric emergency assessment
- Management of chronic childhood conditions

## Clinical Focus

- Common childhood infections (respiratory, gastrointestinal, ENT)
- Asthma and allergic disease in children
- Growth and developmental assessment
- Neonatal jaundice and feeding issues
- Childhood fever evaluation
- Chronic conditions (diabetes, epilepsy, inflammatory bowel disease in children)
