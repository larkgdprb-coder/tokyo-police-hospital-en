## Overview

The Obstetrics & Gynecology Department provides comprehensive care throughout pregnancy and childbirth, as well as diagnosis and treatment of gynecological conditions. We operate a Perinatal Care Center offering high-level maternal and neonatal care.

## Key Services

- Antenatal care and delivery (including high-risk obstetrics)
- Gynecological cancer diagnosis and surgery
- Laparoscopic and minimally invasive gynecological surgery
- Infertility and reproductive health consultations

## Obstetrics

- Antenatal check-ups and screening
- High-risk pregnancy management
- Delivery (vaginal and caesarean section)
- Postpartum care
- Neonatal support (in collaboration with Pediatrics)

## Gynecology

- Cervical cancer screening (Pap smear, HPV testing)
- Endometrial and ovarian cancer surgery
- Uterine fibroids — myomectomy and hysterectomy
- Endometriosis management
- Laparoscopic surgery
- Pelvic floor disorders and prolapse surgery
- Menopausal medicine

## Maternity Classes

We offer regular maternity classes for expectant mothers. Please contact us for the current schedule.
