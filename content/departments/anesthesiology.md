## Overview

The Anesthesiology Department provides safe and effective anesthesia for all surgical procedures performed at Tokyo Police Hospital. Our team of board-certified anesthesiologists manages general anesthesia, regional anesthesia, sedation, and perioperative pain control.

## Services

- General anesthesia for elective and emergency surgery
- Regional anesthesia (spinal, epidural, nerve blocks)
- Sedation for endoscopic and interventional procedures
- Perioperative pain management
- Intensive care unit (ICU) support

## Pain Management

Our anesthesiologists specialize in acute postoperative pain management using:
- Patient-controlled analgesia (PCA)
- Epidural analgesia
- Peripheral nerve blocks
- Multimodal pain therapy

## Pre-Anesthetic Assessment

Patients scheduled for surgery will have a **pre-anesthetic assessment** in advance. Please bring a complete list of your current medications and any records of previous anesthesia.

If you have concerns about anesthesia, please discuss them with your surgeon or contact the anesthesiology department in advance.
