## Overview

The Urology Department provides surgical and non-surgical care for diseases of the kidney, ureter, bladder, prostate, and male reproductive tract. We perform minimally invasive and robotic urological surgery.

## Key Services

- Prostate cancer diagnosis, surveillance, and surgical treatment
- Bladder cancer evaluation and treatment (TURBT, radical cystectomy)
- Kidney stone treatment (extracorporeal shockwave lithotripsy, ureteroscopy)
- Robotic-assisted laparoscopic prostatectomy (da Vinci)

## Clinical Focus

- Prostate cancer — PSA screening, biopsy, surgery, and active surveillance
- Bladder cancer
- Renal cell carcinoma (kidney cancer)
- Urinary stones (nephrolithiasis, ureterolithiasis)
- Benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH)
- Overactive bladder and urinary incontinence
- Male infertility and erectile dysfunction

## Procedures

- Transurethral resection of the prostate (TURP)
- Transurethral resection of bladder tumor (TURBT)
- Robotic radical prostatectomy
- Ureteroscopy and stone fragmentation
- Extracorporeal shockwave lithotripsy (ESWL)
