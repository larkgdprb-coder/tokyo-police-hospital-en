## Overview

The Ophthalmology Department provides comprehensive eye care including medical and surgical management of eye diseases. We offer a full range of diagnostic testing and ophthalmic surgical procedures.

## Key Services

- Cataract surgery (phacoemulsification with IOL implantation)
- Glaucoma diagnosis and management
- Retinal disease evaluation and treatment
- Diabetic eye disease screening and laser treatment

## Clinical Focus

- Cataracts — surgical correction
- Glaucoma — medical and surgical management
- Diabetic retinopathy — laser photocoagulation, intravitreal injections
- Age-related macular degeneration (AMD)
- Retinal detachment surgery
- Corneal disease and dry eye syndrome
- Strabismus and amblyopia (children)
- Uveitis and inflammatory eye disease
- Eyelid conditions (ptosis, entropion, ectropion)

## Procedures

- Cataract surgery (phacoemulsification)
- Laser photocoagulation for diabetic retinopathy
- Intravitreal injections (anti-VEGF therapy)
- Retinal detachment repair
- Trabeculectomy for glaucoma
