## Overview

The Emergency & Critical Care Medicine Department provides 24-hour emergency care for urgent and life-threatening conditions. Our emergency team includes board-certified emergency physicians supported by specialists across all departments.

## Emergency Services

Tokyo Police Hospital's emergency department is available **24 hours a day, 365 days a year**.

**Phone (24 hours): 03-5343-5611**

Please call before arriving whenever possible.

## Conditions Treated

- Cardiac arrest and acute cardiovascular emergencies
- Stroke and neurological emergencies
- Trauma (fractures, head injury, abdominal injury)
- Respiratory emergencies (severe asthma, respiratory failure)
- Acute abdominal conditions
- Severe infections and sepsis
- Overdose and toxicological emergencies
- Obstetric and gynecological emergencies

## After-Hours Outpatient Care

For less critical conditions outside regular outpatient hours, a duty physician is available. An after-hours surcharge (*jikan-gai sentei ryōyōhi*) may apply for non-emergency visits.

→ [After-Hours & Holiday Care](/outpatient/after-hours/)

## Ambulance

If arriving by ambulance, the ambulance crew will contact us in advance. Our emergency team will be prepared upon your arrival.
