## Overview

The Rehabilitation Medicine Department provides comprehensive rehabilitation services to restore function and improve quality of life for patients recovering from illness, injury, or surgery. Our multidisciplinary team includes physical therapists, occupational therapists, and speech-language therapists.

## Key Services

- Physical therapy (PT) — mobility, strength, balance
- Occupational therapy (OT) — daily living activities, upper limb function
- Speech-language therapy (ST) — communication and swallowing
- Cardiac and respiratory rehabilitation

## Clinical Focus

- Post-stroke rehabilitation
- Orthopedic rehabilitation (joint replacement, fractures)
- Neurological rehabilitation (Parkinson's disease, spinal cord injury)
- Cardiac rehabilitation after heart attack or cardiac surgery
- Respiratory rehabilitation for COPD
- Swallowing rehabilitation (dysphagia)
- Post-surgical rehabilitation
- Pediatric rehabilitation

## Inpatient and Outpatient Rehabilitation

We provide rehabilitation services to both inpatients (on the wards) and outpatients attending regular therapy sessions.

Outpatient rehabilitation requires a physician's referral and prior appointment.
