## Overview

The General Internal Medicine Department provides comprehensive diagnosis and management of a broad range of conditions affecting internal organs. We serve as the initial point of assessment for complex or multi-system illnesses and coordinate care with specialist departments as needed.

## Key Services

- Diagnosis and management of fever of unknown origin, fatigue, and general systemic symptoms
- Multidisciplinary management of patients with multiple chronic conditions
- Pre-operative internal medicine evaluation
- Management of infectious diseases, metabolic disorders, and chronic illnesses

## Clinical Focus

- Hypertension, dyslipidemia, diabetes mellitus
- Infectious diseases and fever workup
- Anemia and general blood disorders
- Coordination with subspecialty departments for complex cases
