## Overview

The Orthopedic Surgery Department provides surgical and non-surgical treatment for musculoskeletal conditions including fractures, joint disease, spinal disorders, and sports injuries.

## Key Services

- Joint replacement surgery (hip, knee)
- Fracture treatment and trauma surgery
- Spine surgery (herniated disc, spinal stenosis)
- Sports medicine and arthroscopic surgery

## Clinical Focus

- Osteoarthritis and joint replacement (hip, knee, shoulder)
- Fractures and orthopedic trauma
- Lumbar disc herniation and spinal stenosis
- Scoliosis and spinal deformity
- Rotator cuff tears and shoulder disorders
- Sports injuries (ligament tears, meniscal injuries)
- Osteoporosis and fragility fractures
- Hand and peripheral nerve conditions

## Procedures

- Total hip and knee replacement
- Arthroscopic surgery (knee, shoulder)
- Spinal fusion and decompression
- Fracture fixation (plates, screws, nails)
- Casting and conservative management
