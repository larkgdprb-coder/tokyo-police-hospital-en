## About orthopedic surgery

Our department covers a wide range of orthopedic diseases, excluding malignant tumors, such as trauma and diseases of bones, joints, and ligaments, as well as strangulation neuropathy. Approximately 1,000 surgeries are performed each year. By taking advantage of the advantages of being a general hospital and collaborating with other departments, we are able to safely undergo surgery even for seriously ill patients, such as those who require dialysis or those with heart disease. In addition, by collaborating with nearby rehabilitation hospitals and clinics, seamless, continuous treatment is possible.

## Main diseases treated by orthopedics

- Trauma to bones and joints such as fractures, dislocations, ligament injuries, and tendon ruptures Age-related changes in joints Knee osteoarthritis, hip osteoarthritis, shoulder periarthritis, etc. Sports-related disorders Recurrent shoulder dislocations, knee cruciate ligament injuries, elbow disorders, meniscal injuries, ankle ligament injuries Foot disorders Hallux valgus, flat feet, ankle osteoarthritis Hand disorders Tendonitis, digital osteoarthritis Peripheral neuropathy Carpal tunnel syndrome, cubital tunnel syndrome, tarsal tunnel syndrome, etc. Metabolic diseases, inflammatory diseases, osteoporosis, septic arthritis, etc. Diseases of the cervical and lumbar vertebrae

Cervical or lumbar disc herniation, lumbar spinal canal stenosis, cervical myelopathy, vertebral compression fractures, etc.

## Treatment contents and characteristics

We have established the following two specialized outpatient clinics and would like to introduce them to you.

### 1. Artificial joint center specialty outpatient clinic

We plan surgical procedures for artificial joints, mainly hip and knee joints, and provide follow-up. We perform approximately 180 to 200 joint replacement surgeries a year. Even patients with complications such as dialysis or cardiovascular disease can undergo surgery with peace of mind, as we work in collaboration with each center and anesthesiology department.

### 2. Sports injury specialist outpatient clinic (Thursday/Friday) AM Nishihara

This is a specialized outpatient clinic that primarily examines patients who require arthroscopic surgery for sports injuries to the knee and shoulder joints. In particular, we specialize in treating anterior cruciate ligament injuries and meniscus injuries in the knee. In the shoulder area, we specialize in treating repetitive shoulder dislocations and rotator cuff injuries. Recently, the surgical time for arthroscopic anterior cruciate ligament reconstruction is about one and a half hours.

## How to use the orthopedic outpatient clinic

- As a general rule, a letter of introduction is required for first-time patients.
- If you have a referral letter, we will make a reservation for you, so please contact our hospital collaboration office from the medical institution or yourself.
- All the doctors in charge of initial consultations in our department are well-versed in general orthopedic surgery, so please use our general initial consultations with confidence.
- If surgery, special treatment, or testing is required, you will be asked to make an appointment to see a specialized outpatient doctor.
