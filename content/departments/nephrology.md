## Overview

The Nephrology & Metabolism Department specializes in the diagnosis and management of kidney diseases and metabolic disorders. We operate the Blood Purification Center, providing hemodialysis and related therapies.

## Key Services

- Management of chronic kidney disease (CKD) and prevention of progression
- Hemodialysis and peritoneal dialysis
- Diabetic nephropathy and hypertensive nephropathy management
- Electrolyte and acid-base disorder management

## Clinical Focus

- Chronic kidney disease (all stages)
- Acute kidney injury
- Glomerulonephritis and nephrotic syndrome
- Dialysis management (hemodialysis and peritoneal dialysis)
- Diabetic nephropathy
- Hypertensive nephropathy
- Electrolyte disorders (sodium, potassium, calcium abnormalities)

## Blood Purification Center

Our Blood Purification Center provides:
- Regular hemodialysis sessions
- Continuous renal replacement therapy (CRRT) for critically ill patients
- Plasmapheresis and LDL apheresis
