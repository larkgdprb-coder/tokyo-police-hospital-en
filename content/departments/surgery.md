## Overview

The Surgery Department specializes in surgical treatment of gastrointestinal tract diseases, including minimally invasive laparoscopic and robotic surgery. We perform high volumes of cancer surgeries and emergency abdominal operations.

## Key Services

- Laparoscopic and open surgery for gastric, colorectal, and liver cancers
- Robotic-assisted surgery (da Vinci system)
- Emergency surgery for acute abdominal conditions
- Hernia repair and elective abdominal procedures

## Clinical Focus

- Gastric cancer surgery
- Colorectal cancer surgery
- Liver, gallbladder, and pancreatic surgery (hepato-biliary-pancreatic)
- Esophageal surgery
- Appendectomy and emergency abdominal surgery
- Inguinal and abdominal wall hernia repair
- Bariatric surgery consultation

## Surgical Approaches

- Laparoscopic (minimally invasive) surgery
- Robotic-assisted surgery (da Vinci Xi)
- Open surgery for complex cases
- Emergency surgery
