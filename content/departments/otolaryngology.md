## Overview

The Otolaryngology (ENT) Department diagnoses and treats conditions of the ear, nose, throat, head, and neck. We offer both medical and surgical management for a wide range of conditions.

## Key Services

- Hearing evaluation and treatment of ear disorders
- Endoscopic nasal and sinus surgery (FESS)
- Head and neck cancer surgery
- Voice and swallowing disorders

## Clinical Focus

**Ear:**
- Hearing loss evaluation and hearing aids
- Otitis media and chronic ear disease
- Tinnitus and vertigo / dizziness (BPPV, Ménière's disease)

**Nose & Sinuses:**
- Allergic rhinitis and nasal polyps
- Chronic sinusitis — functional endoscopic sinus surgery (FESS)
- Nasal septum deviation and turbinate hypertrophy

**Throat & Voice:**
- Tonsil disorders and tonsillectomy
- Vocal cord polyps and nodules
- Sleep-disordered breathing and snoring
- Swallowing disorders (dysphagia)

**Head & Neck:**
- Head and neck cancer (thyroid, salivary gland, larynx, pharynx)
- Thyroid and parathyroid surgery
- Neck masses and lymph node evaluation
