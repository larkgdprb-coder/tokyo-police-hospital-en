## Daily Routine

During your stay, each day will generally follow this schedule:

| Time | Activity |
|---|---|
| 7:45 | Breakfast served |
| 12:00 | Lunch served |
| 18:00 | Dinner served |
| Morning | Doctor's rounds |
| Throughout the day | Nursing care, therapy sessions, tests |
| 21:00 | Lights out (please rest quietly after this time) |
| 22:00 | Ward quieted; visitor departures required |

## Meals

Hospital meals are planned by our registered dietitians and tailored to your medical condition and dietary needs. Meals are served three times a day. If you have food allergies, religious dietary requirements, or other concerns, please inform the dietitian or nursing staff.

## Rental Services

The following rental services are available for inpatients (fees apply):

| Service | Notes |
|---|---|
| **Bedside TV** | Paid television at your bedside (prepaid card system) |
| **Bedside Refrigerator** | For storing personal drinks and items approved by staff |
| **Wi-Fi / Internet access** | Available throughout the hospital. Ask staff for details. |
| **Pajamas / Hospital Gown rental** | Laundry included |
| **Towel rental** | Changed regularly |

Please contact the ward staff or the amenity service desk for rental details and pricing.

## Laundry Service

A **laundry service** is available for inpatients. Staff can provide details about scheduling and fees. Alternatively, family members may take personal laundry home.

A coin-operated laundry room is also available on designated floors for patient use during appropriate hours.

## Telephone & Communication

- **Mobile phones** may be used in designated areas only. See [Mobile Phone Policy]({{< relref "/support/mobile" >}}) for details.
- **Public phones** are available in the lobby area.
- Calls to the nursing station from outside: **03-5343-5611** (main) → specify the ward

## Leaving the Ward (Out-Pass)

If you wish to temporarily leave the ward or the hospital during your stay, you must:
1. Obtain **permission from your attending physician**
2. Notify the nursing staff before leaving and upon return
3. Follow any conditions set by your doctor (e.g., time limits, restrictions)

Leaving without permission may affect your treatment plan and insurance coverage.

## Valuables

We strongly advise against bringing large amounts of cash or valuables during hospitalization. The hospital is not responsible for loss or theft of personal belongings. Bedside lockers are available for securing small items.

## Smoking Policy

Tokyo Police Hospital is a **completely non-smoking facility**. Smoking is strictly prohibited anywhere on hospital grounds, including outdoor areas. This policy applies to all patients, visitors, and staff.
