## Suggestions & Feedback

Tokyo Police Hospital values every comment, suggestion, and concern from our patients, families, and visitors. Your feedback is essential for helping us improve our services and the quality of care we provide.

## Suggestion Boxes

**Suggestion boxes** are located at the following locations throughout the hospital:
- Main lobby (1st Floor, near Information Desk)
- Outpatient waiting areas (2F, 3F)
- Ward entrances (5F–9F)

Submission forms and pens are available next to each box. You may submit anonymously, or include your contact information if you would like a response.

## How to Submit Feedback

### In Person
Speak with any nurse, reception staff, or ward charge nurse. For more formal concerns, ask to speak with the **Patient Relations Coordinator** or the **Ward Manager**.

### By Suggestion Box
Complete a suggestion form (available at all suggestion boxes) and deposit it in the nearest box. Forms are collected and reviewed regularly by hospital management.

### By Phone
- **Main Reception:** 03-5343-5611
- **Hours:** Monday – Saturday, 8:00 – 17:00

## How We Handle Feedback

1. All submissions are reviewed by the Patient Relations team and relevant department managers.
2. Suggestions requiring a response (where contact information is provided) will receive a reply within **approximately 2 weeks**.
3. Themes from feedback are reported to the hospital's Quality Improvement Committee.
4. We publish a summary of patient feedback and our responses on the hospital's notice boards and in internal reports.

## Compliments

We love to hear about positive experiences! If a member of our staff went above and beyond for you, please let us know. Compliments are shared with the relevant staff members and their department heads.

## Complaints

If you have a serious concern about your care, please:
1. Speak with the ward charge nurse or department manager first.
2. If the concern is not resolved, contact the **Patient Relations Desk** (1F).
3. If you are still unsatisfied, you may contact the **Tokyo Metropolitan Medical Safety Support Center** (external body) for independent mediation.

We treat every complaint seriously and are committed to reviewing it thoroughly and responding promptly.

## Your Voice Matters

Even small comments can lead to significant improvements. Whether it's a suggestion about the waiting room environment, the clarity of signage, or the quality of a meal — we want to hear from you. Thank you for helping us serve you better.
