## Please let us know your opinion

At our hospital, we have set up "suggestion boxes" in the outpatient hall and in each ward to hear patients' wishes and impressions about the hospital, in an effort to further improve our medical services. We will consider all comments received within our hospital and strive to make improvements. If you notice anything, please feel free to let us know your opinion. We will use this as a reference for creating better hospitals. We look forward to your opinions and cooperation.
