## Cancer consultation support counter




At the Cancer Consultation Support Desk, specialized nurses and medical social workers will listen to your concerns about your illness, treatment methods, treatment side effects, future direction of treatment, recuperation life, treatment costs, etc., and will work with you to consider and respond to your concerns. If you are interested, please contact the Cancer Consultation Support Desk (Admission/Discharge Support/Patient Consultation Center on the second floor of the new building).
