## Notice regarding medical record disclosure application

### For those who wish to have their medical information disclosed

If you would like to apply for disclosure, please check this "notice", bring the necessary documents, and call the general information desk on the 1st floor of our hospital. Please note that it will take approximately 10 days to 2 weeks from the time we receive your application until the information is disclosed. Please note that in this case, there may be cases where disclosure may not be made, such as in cases where there is a risk of significant damage to the patient's physical and mental condition. In addition, we have established a prescribed fee, so please be aware of this before applying.

#### 1 How to apply

Please visit our hospital in person and fill out the required information on the "Medical Information Disclosure Application Form" prescribed by our hospital. At that time, please bring (1) a medical examination ticket, (2) a seal, and (3) an identity verification document issued by a public institution (one if it has a photo, such as a driver's license, passport, or My Number card; two if it does not have a photo, such as an insurance card).

#### 2. Persons who can apply for disclosure

As a general rule, the applicant is the patient himself/herself. If the application is made by a representative such as a family member, (1) a power of attorney from the patient, (2) a copy of the patient's identity verification document issued by a public institution, and (3) a document proving the relationship with the patient (issued within three months, such as a family register). *If the application is made by a sub-agent, such as a clerk at a law firm, please contact us as there are other required documents.

#### 3 Materials that can be disclosed

Medical records (medical records, nursing records, etc.), various test records, image records (X-rays, etc.)

#### 4 Cost of disclosure

| item | unit | Fee |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Medical information disclosure fee | per application | 3,000 yen (3,300 yen including tax) |
| Copies of medical records stored in paper charts (★) | per piece | 30 yen (33 yen including tax) |
| Copies of medical records stored in electronic medical records | per piece | 20 yen (22 yen including tax) |
| Importing images stored in electronic medical records (DVD, etc.) | per piece | 1,000 yen (1,100 yen including tax) |

◇Depending on the storage medium, work may be outsourced to an external contractor. In that case, shipping charges will be incurred, and the actual cost will be charged in addition to the above costs. *According to Article 24 of the Medical Practitioners Act, medical records are kept for 5 years after the completion of medical treatment, so medical records may not exist.
