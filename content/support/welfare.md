The Medical and Welfare Consultation Office employs social workers* who provide consultation on all lifestyle issues related to medical treatment for patients and their families. *Social worker: A specialized occupation that deals with consultations related to daily life caused by illness or disability.

- Consultation regarding medical expenses If your medical expenses have become expensive or you are concerned about medical expenses, etc.
- Consultation regarding medical treatment after discharge: Direction after discharge, home medical care, transfer to hospital, admission to a facility, etc.
- Consultation regarding the use of various welfare systems and public systems Nursing care insurance, independence support medical care, disability certificate, welfare, etc.
- Consultations about home care, explanations about home services, use of visiting nurses and doctors, etc.
- Consultations regarding facility use and admission, facility selection, costs, application methods, etc.
- Consultation about welfare equipment and home renovations, how to use them, fees, etc.

We also provide consultation on various issues arising from illness or disability.

◎Reception hours: 8:30-17:00 (Monday-Saturday *Excluding Sundays, public holidays, and year-end and New Year holidays) Consultations are generally by appointment only. Reservations are given priority, so if you would like to meet us, please make an appointment in advance by visiting our clinic or by telephone. (Outpatient/hospitalized)

◇Consultations are free. The contents of the consultation will be kept confidential. Please feel free to contact us.
