## For patients who have a medical information form (letter of introduction)

If you have a medical information letter (letter of introduction) from another medical institution, you will not be charged the 7,700 yen medical treatment fee for your first visit. If you have been diagnosed by another medical institution as needing treatment at our hospital and have provided us with a medical information form, you can make an appointment with our hospital over the phone. Once the patient's symptoms have stabilized and the patient has recovered to the point where medical treatment at our hospital is no longer necessary, the patient will receive further treatment at the family medical institution that referred the patient.

Please have your letter of introduction ready and contact the Medical Cooperation Office.
