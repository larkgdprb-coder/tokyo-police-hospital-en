## Before Your First Visit

If you are visiting Tokyo Police Hospital for the first time, please review the information below carefully before your appointment.

## Opening Hours

| | Time |
|---|---|
| **Main Entrance Opens** | 7:45 |
| **Reception Hours (Morning)** | 8:00 – 11:30 |
| **Reception Hours (Afternoon)** | 12:30 – 16:00 |

## Required Documents

Please bring the following items to the reception desk on your first visit:

1. **Health Insurance Card** (or MyNumber Card / Individual Number Card for online insurance verification)
2. **Referral Letter** from your regular physician or clinic *(strongly recommended)*
3. **Medical history summary** (if available)
4. **List of current medications** (prescription notebooks, medication lists, or actual medications)
5. **Any relevant previous test results** (X-rays, blood test results, etc.)
6. **Emergency contact information**

## Initial Consultation Surcharge

In accordance with national healthcare guidelines, patients who visit **without a referral letter** from another medical institution are required to pay an **initial consultation surcharge of ¥7,700** (tax included) in addition to normal medical fees.

This surcharge is set by the government for designated hospitals to encourage appropriate use of medical resources and promote the role of primary care physicians.

### Exemptions from the Surcharge

The ¥7,700 surcharge does **not** apply in the following situations:

- Patients arriving by ambulance or other emergency transport
- Patients requiring urgent or emergency treatment
- Patients referred from occupational health settings
- Patients referred from other hospitals or clinics (with a referral letter)
- Patients with conditions unique to this hospital (second opinion, specialized treatment)
- Children under 6 years of age (varies by situation)
- Patients with certain designated conditions

## MyNumber Card / Online Insurance Verification

We support online insurance eligibility verification using your **MyNumber Card** (Individual Number Card). Bringing your MyNumber card speeds up the registration process and eliminates the need to re-submit your insurance card at every visit.

If you do not have a MyNumber card, please bring your current health insurance card.

## Reception Procedure

1. Proceed to the **First Visit Reception Counter** on the 1st floor.
2. Complete and submit the new patient registration form.
3. Present your insurance card / MyNumber card.
4. Submit your referral letter (if applicable).
5. Wait to be called and proceed to the relevant department.

## Important Notes

- Please arrive early, as wait times may vary depending on the clinic.
- If you have a pre-scheduled appointment, please arrive at least 15 minutes before your appointment time.
- Afternoon clinic is by **appointment only** (except Pediatrics).
- For urgent medical concerns, call **03-5343-5611** before visiting.
