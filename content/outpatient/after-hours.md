## After-Hours & Holiday Outpatient Care

Tokyo Police Hospital provides emergency outpatient care outside of regular clinic hours, including nights and holidays.

### Contact

**Main Phone: 03-5343-5611 (24 hours)**

Please call ahead before visiting. A duty physician will respond to emergency cases.

### Regular Hours (for reference)

| | Hours |
|---|---|
| Reception | Mon–Sat 8:00–11:30 / 12:30–16:00 |
| Clinic | Mon–Sat 8:30–12:00 / 13:00–16:30 |

### Closed Days (Regular Outpatient)

- Sundays
- Public Holidays
- Year-end / New Year (December 29 – January 3)

### Important Notes

- After-hours care is intended for emergency situations.
- For non-urgent conditions, please visit during regular outpatient hours.
- Please bring your insurance card (or MyNumber card) and any current prescriptions or medications.
- If arriving by ambulance, please contact the hospital in advance when possible.

### After-Hours Surcharge

An after-hours selection fee (*jikan-gai sentei ryōyōhi*) may apply for non-emergency visits outside of regular hours.

Please call **03-5343-5611** for the latest information on after-hours availability.
