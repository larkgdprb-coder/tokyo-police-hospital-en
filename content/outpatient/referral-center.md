## About priority medical institutions for referrals

Our hospital is positioned as a regional core hospital that possesses advanced medical equipment and facilities, and has been selected as a ``Priority Medical Institution for Referrals'', which focuses on treating patients referred from clinics and other hospitals.

### If this is your first visit, please bring a letter of introduction.

If you come to the hospital without a referral letter, you will be required to pay a selected medical treatment fee (special fee) in addition to the co-payment (30% payment, etc.). *This also applies to patients currently attending our hospital who visit other departments for other symptoms. Please note that if you bring a letter of introduction, you will not be charged the selected medical treatment fee (special fee).

◇For information on priority medical institutions for referrals, please see the Ministry of Health, Labor and Welfare homepage (external link).

### After treatment, we will refer you back to your primary care physician.

In an effort to provide seamless medical care from hospitalization to home, the Ministry of Health, Labor and Welfare has instructed us to separate the medical functions of clinics (family doctors) and large hospitals, and to have hospitals and clinics collaborate in providing medical examinations.
