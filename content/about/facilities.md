## Hospital Facilities & Amenities

Tokyo Police Hospital's modern facility offers a range of amenities to support patients, families, and visitors during their time at the hospital.

## Convenience Store

**Natural Lawson** (Located on the 1st Floor)

| | |
|---|---|
| **Hours** | 6:00 – 21:00 (daily) |
| **ATM** | Available |

Natural Lawson offers a selection of fresh foods, snacks, beverages, personal care items, and daily necessities. An ATM is available for cash withdrawals (fees may apply for non-Lawson bank cards).

## Café

**Tully's Coffee** (Located in the 1st Floor lobby)

| | |
|---|---|
| **Hours** | Monday – Saturday, 7:30 – 18:00 |
| **Closed** | Sundays and Public Holidays |

Enjoy freshly brewed coffee, teas, and light meals in a comfortable setting. Tully's Coffee provides a welcoming space for patients, families, and visitors.

## Barber Shop

**Oasis** (Barber / Hairdresser)

| | |
|---|---|
| **Hours** | 9:00 – 17:00 |

"Oasis" offers haircuts and basic hair care services for inpatients and outpatients. Advance booking is recommended. Please ask the ward staff or the 1st Floor information desk for details.

## Hospital Restaurant

**Hospital Restaurant** (Staff and visitor dining facility)

| | |
|---|---|
| **Hours** | 11:00 – 15:00 (Weekdays only) |
| **Closed** | Weekends and Public Holidays |

A cafeteria-style restaurant offering hot meals, set lunches, and a variety of menu options. Open to hospital staff, patients (outpatient), and visitors.

## Parking

| | |
|---|---|
| **Capacity** | 84 spaces |
| **Hours** | 24 hours / 7 days a week |
| **General Rate** | ¥200 per 30 minutes |
| **Patient Rate** | ¥100 per 30 minutes (with parking validation at reception) |
| **Disabled Parking** | Free for holders of a Disability Handbook (*Shōgai-sha Techō*) |

Please obtain a parking ticket validation stamp at the hospital reception or cashier to qualify for the discounted patient rate.

## Bicycle & Motorcycle Parking

Bicycle and motorcycle parking is available on hospital grounds at no charge. Please use the designated areas.

## Wheelchair & Stroller Loan

Wheelchairs (12 units) and strollers (2 units) are available free of charge for loan at the **General Information Desk** on the 1st Floor.

## ATM

An ATM is available inside the Natural Lawson convenience store on the 1st Floor (hours: 6:00–21:00).

## Public Telephones

Public telephones are located near the 1st Floor lobby for patient and visitor use.

## Prayer / Quiet Room

A quiet room is available for rest, prayer, or reflection. Please ask at the information desk for the location.

## Accessibility

Our facility is fully accessible:
- Elevators are available on all floors
- Accessible toilets on all floors
- Wheelchair-accessible entrance and pathways throughout the building
- Hearing loop systems in selected consultation areas

For any accessibility needs, please inform the information desk staff and they will be pleased to assist you.
