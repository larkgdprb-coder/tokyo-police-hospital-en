## philosophy

We strive day and night to improve the quality of medical care and patient satisfaction.

## Basic policy

- We respect the rights and will of patients and practice medical care from the patient's perspective.
- We always strive to improve medical morale and the level of medical care, and provide high-quality and reliable medical care.
- We contribute to the development of local medical care based on the trust of local residents.
- We support the workplace through medical care and contribute to the safety of society.
- We promote sound management so that patients can concentrate on their treatment with peace of mind.

## Infection control guidelines

Tokyo Police Hospital takes measures against in-hospital infections based on the hospital's basic policy, with the goal of preventing medical-related infections before they occur, providing a safe and comfortable hospital life, and quickly controlling and ending infectious diseases once they occur.

## Medical safety management guidelines

The Tokyo Police Hospital will promote safe and appropriate medical operations by establishing a medical safety management system to prevent medical accidents, and by responding quickly and accurately in the event of a medical accident.
