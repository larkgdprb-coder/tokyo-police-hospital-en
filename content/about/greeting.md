## Director's Message

**Shunji Hasegawa, MD, PhD**
*Director, Tokyo Police Hospital*

---

On behalf of everyone at Tokyo Police Hospital, I would like to extend a warm welcome to all our patients, visitors, and partners.

Tokyo Police Hospital was founded in 1929 (Showa Year 4) in Chiyoda-ku, Tokyo, as a dedicated medical institution to support officers of the Tokyo Metropolitan Police Department. In 2008, we relocated to our current location in Nakano-ku, a modern, purpose-built facility designed to meet the medical needs of the 21st century.

Over the course of nearly a century, our hospital has grown significantly. We now operate **30 clinical departments** and **10 specialized centers**, providing comprehensive medical services not only to police officers and their families but also to the wider community.

### Our Mission

Our fundamental mission is to pursue excellence in the quality of medical care we provide, and to ensure that every patient feels safe, respected, and well-cared for. We believe that medicine is ultimately a deeply human endeavor — built on trust between patients and caregivers.

We receive more than **5,000 emergency patients annually**, and our teams work tirelessly around the clock to ensure the best possible outcomes. Whether a patient comes to us for a routine outpatient visit, a complex surgical procedure, or urgent emergency care, our goal is always the same: to provide compassionate, evidence-based care.

### Advanced Medicine, Human Touch

In recent years, we have invested substantially in advanced medical technology, including the **da Vinci Xi robotic surgical system**, **PET/CT imaging**, **3.0 Tesla MRI**, and **linear accelerator radiation therapy**. These tools allow our physicians to diagnose and treat conditions with greater precision and less discomfort for our patients.

At the same time, we firmly believe that technology must always serve the human relationship at the core of medicine. We strive to listen carefully to each patient, explain treatment options clearly, and involve patients fully in decisions about their care.

### Looking Ahead

As healthcare continues to evolve rapidly, we are committed to ongoing education, research, and quality improvement. We actively seek and maintain accreditation from major medical societies and work to attract and develop talented healthcare professionals.

We will continue to earn your trust through dedication, integrity, and excellence in every aspect of our work.

I look forward to welcoming you to Tokyo Police Hospital.

*Shunji Hasegawa, MD, PhD*
