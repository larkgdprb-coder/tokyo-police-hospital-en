### fundamental rights

Equal access to high-quality and safe medical care We respect child patients as human beings and guarantee their right to receive good medical care.

### Right to information

You can receive sufficient explanation and information

### The right to self-determination and freedom of choice

You can choose or refuse treatment based on your own will.

### Right to information protection

Personal medical information and privacy are protected

### responsibilities and obligations

Please refrain from any behavior that may interfere with the treatment of other patients or the work of medical professionals.
