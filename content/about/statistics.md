## Hospital Performance Indicators

In the interest of transparency, Tokyo Police Hospital publishes key performance indicators annually. These statistics provide insight into the scope and quality of care we deliver.

> The figures below are based on the most recently available annual data. Updated statistics are published each year on the hospital's Japanese website.

| Indicator | Value |
|---|---|
| Licensed Beds | 415 (general ward) |
| Average Bed Occupancy Rate | Approx. 80–85% |
| Average Length of Stay | Approx. 9–11 days |
| Annual Inpatient Admissions | Approx. 10,000–12,000 |
| Nurse-to-Patient Ratio (Acute Ward) | 1:7 (Type 1) |
