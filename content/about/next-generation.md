We will formulate the following action plan in order to create an employment environment where employees can demonstrate their abilities, balance work and life, and create a comfortable working environment.

## 1. planning period

Five years from April 1, 2020 to March 31, 2021

## 2. Content

### (1) Goal 1

Increase the rate of men taking childcare leave to 70% or more within the plan period. <Measures> ●From June 2020: Inform employees who wish to take childcare leave, etc. individually and confirm their intentions. ●From September 2020: We will individually explain systems such as childcare leave to employees whose spouses are planning to give birth. ●From December 2020: Promote awareness of systems related to childcare leave and nursing care leave.

### (2) Goal 2

For each month within the plan period, the number of employees who work overtime in courtrooms for 45 hours or more per month will be reduced by 10%. <Measures> ● From May 2020: Understanding overtime hours ● From October 2020: Promoting no-overtime days (held twice a month) ●From January 2020: Identifying problems in each department with high overtime hours ●Started operation from April 2020 with the goal of reducing overtime hours to 45 hours or less
