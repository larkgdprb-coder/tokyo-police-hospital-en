## Partnership between patients and healthcare providers

As a regional core hospital that provides emergency medical care (relief activities), acute phase medical care, and advanced medical care, Tokyo Police Hospital's ``philosophy'' is to ``work day and night to improve the quality of medical care and patient satisfaction.''

## Basic guidelines for appropriate decision support

At Tokyo Police Hospital, our basic policy is to provide medical care that respects the wishes of each patient by providing appropriate explanations and thorough discussions with patients, their families, etc. through a medical and care team made up of a multidisciplinary team, so that all patients can receive the best medical care and care possible for them.

## Informed consent guidelines

Informed consent means ``When receiving treatment, a patient receives a sufficient explanation from a doctor about the examination, treatment policy, and method related to his or her disease, and after understanding and being satisfied with the contents, selects the appropriate medical treatment and receives treatment.''
