## Partnership Program

Tokyo Police Hospital actively fosters partnerships with local clinics, community hospitals, and healthcare providers throughout the Tokyo area. Our partnership program is designed to ensure seamless, continuous care for patients across all levels of the healthcare system.

## Why Partnerships Matter

Modern healthcare is most effective when patients can move smoothly between different levels of care:

- **Primary Care (Local Clinics):** Routine health management, preventive care, chronic disease monitoring
- **Secondary Care (General Hospitals):** Diagnosis and treatment of conditions requiring specialist expertise
- **Tertiary Care (Specialized Centers):** Complex surgery, advanced technology, intensive care

Tokyo Police Hospital occupies the secondary and tertiary care levels. Our partnerships ensure that patients have access to appropriate primary care and that transitions between care levels are well-coordinated.

## For Referring Physicians

We welcome referrals from general practitioners, family physicians, and community clinics. As a partner institution, we offer:

- **Priority appointment scheduling** for referred patients
- **Prompt written feedback** to referring physicians after specialist consultations
- **Return referrals** for patients who have completed specialist treatment and are ready for ongoing management at the community level
- **Joint case conferences** for complex cases
- **Continuing medical education (CME) events** for community physicians

### How to Refer a Patient

1. Contact our **Medical Liaison Office**: 03-5343-5760 / 03-5343-5762
2. Prepare a **referral letter** with relevant medical history, current medications, and reason for referral
3. Fax referral documents to: 03-5343-5763
4. We will contact the patient to schedule an appointment

## For Patients

If you are currently receiving care from a local clinic and need specialist-level evaluation or treatment, ask your doctor about a referral to Tokyo Police Hospital.

A referral letter:
- Waives the ¥7,700 initial consultation surcharge
- Ensures your local doctor is kept informed of your specialist care
- Facilitates a smooth return to your local clinic after treatment

## Community Education

We regularly hold public seminars and community health events covering topics such as cardiovascular disease, cancer prevention, diabetes management, and emergency preparedness. Follow our hospital announcements for upcoming events.
