## About Our Hospital

Tokyo Police Hospital (*Tōkyō Keisatsu Byōin*) is a general hospital operated by the **General Foundation Jikkei-kai** (*Ippan Zaidan Hōjin Jikkei-kai*). Originally established in 1929 to serve the Tokyo Metropolitan Police Department, the hospital now provides advanced medical care to police officers, their families, and the general public.
