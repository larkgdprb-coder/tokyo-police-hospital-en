## Access & Directions

## Hospital Address

**Tokyo Police Hospital (東京警察病院)**
4-22-1 Nakano, Nakano-ku, Tokyo 164-8541

- **Phone:** 03-5343-5611
- **Fax:** 03-5343-5612

## By Train

The most convenient way to reach the hospital is by train to **Nakano Station**.

| Line | Station | Exit | Walk |
|---|---|---|---|
| JR Chuo Line | Nakano Station | **North Exit** | Approx. 10 min walk |
| Tokyo Metro Tozai Line | Nakano Station | **North Exit** | Approx. 10 min walk |

From Nakano Station North Exit, turn left and head north along the main road. The hospital is a large 9-story building clearly visible from the road.

## By Bus

Kanto Bus (*Kantō Bus*) stops near the hospital:

| Bus Stop | Notes |
|---|---|
| **Tokyo Police Hospital Main Gate** (東京警察病院正門前) | Direct stop at main entrance |
| **Tokyo Police Hospital North Gate** (東京警察病院北門前) | Stop at the north entrance |

Please check the Kanto Bus route map for current schedules and routes from Nakano Station.

## By Car / Parking

| | |
|---|---|
| **Parking Capacity** | 84 spaces |
| **Hours** | 24 hours / 7 days a week |
| **General Rate** | ¥200 per 30 minutes |
| **Patient / Visitor Rate** | ¥100 per 30 minutes (with validation at hospital reception) |
| **Disabled Parking** | Free for holders of a Disability Handbook (*Shōgai-sha Techō*) |

To receive the discounted patient rate, please have your parking ticket validated at the reception desk or cashier after your appointment.

**Directions from the south (Koshu Kaido):** Turn north at the Nakano intersection. The hospital is on the right after approximately 500m.

**Navigation:** Enter "東京警察病院" or "Tokyo Police Hospital" in your car navigation system, or use the postal code 164-8541.

## By Bicycle or Motorcycle

Bicycle and motorcycle parking is available on hospital grounds at no charge. Please use the designated parking areas near the entrance.

## Accessibility

Tokyo Police Hospital is fully accessible:
- Fully step-free from the main entrance to all patient care areas
- Lift access to all floors
- Accessible restrooms on all floors
- Wheelchair loan available at the Information Desk (1F)

If you require assistance on arrival, please ask any hospital staff member or contact reception in advance.

## Nearby Landmarks

- Nakano Station (JR/Tokyo Metro) – 10 min walk
- Nakano Broadway – 8 min walk (southwest of station)
- Nakano Shotengai (shopping street) – en route from station
